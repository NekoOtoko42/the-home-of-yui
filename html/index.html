Hello!

Written by Yui James between the 9th & 12th of July 2021.

Hello! My name is Yui, though you probably know me by a different name, a name I do not wish to go by anymore. I'm not to sure about my middle name, but I don't know for now.

The reason for this is because I do not feel the name fits me, as a person, and is appropriate for who I am in terms of gender.

In terms of gender, I am non-binary, more specifically I am more feminine than I am masculine, there are other contributions to my gender, but all that really matters to you is that I am closer to female than I am male, but I am not female or male.

This means that, yes, I am trans, specifically transfeminine, and I feel like I wish to go on hrt but that can be discussed later ig.

Though I am not female (referenced as that is the gender these pronouns are most commonly used with), I wish to go by the pronouns She/Her

You may be having trouble understanding what a non-binary gender is, and that may be due to the fact you misunderstand the meaning of gender, if that is the case, then please consult this heavily researched publication The Gender Dysphoria Bible

In terms of sexuality I am omnisexual, I have dated men, women, and am comfortable with dating any gender
